Miliary Officers Association of America (MOAA)

Virtual chapter for USPHS commissioned officers

.hidden{ display : none; } Military Officers Association of America Presentation: See more

Thank you very much for allowing Annie and me to participate in your call today. I am providing below some updated info on our initiative for the virtual chapter.

First, please understand that MOAA’s Board of Directors needs to approve this USPHS Virtual Chapter as an official MOAA affiliate before we can actually advertise it as a MOAA Virtual Chapter. We submitted all required documents for approval on 28 Jul 2020 and the MOAA Board will address the request - with almost guaranteed approval - at its next meeting in Oct 2020. In the meantime, we continue to advertise by word-of-mouth and recruit charter members.

Things are progressing well - so far, we have 27 charter members including the following initial Board of Directors:

Pres Lam, Annie, LCDR ACT (CA)

VP Maeda, Judith CAPT RET (AZ)

Ses/Tres Matusovsky, Vlada CDR ACT (PA)

Dir Rhynes, Sharon CDR ACT (GA)

Dir Hughley, Crystal CDR ACT (SC)

A few details on this “proposed” USPHS Virtual Chapter:

Mission Statement:

To promote the purposes and objectives of the Military Officers Association of America (MOAA); to foster fraternal relations among active, retired, and former officers of the U.S. Public Health Service; protect the rights and interests of active duty, retired, and reserve component personnel of the uniformed services for members and, dependents and survivors; and to advocate for the protection, promotion, and advancement of the health and safety of the nation.

Some administrative items:

Dues: NO - There will not be any dues or fees for this MOAA USPHS virtual chapter

MOAA Membership: YES - membership with national MOAA is required (very easy process - we will assist)

Three categories available: Basic (free - electronic), Premium (annual), and Life

Go to following link to lean more: https://www.moaa.org/content/join-moaa/why-join-moaa/

Chapter Membership: Membership with the nearest geographic MOAA chapter would still be strongly encouraged

We cordially invite members of the USPHS Commissioned Corps to join as charter members and would be honored to have them do so.
